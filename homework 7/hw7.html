Homework 7: Stadium Capacities in North Carolina

Below is a proportional map of the different capacities of stadiums throughout North Carolina. I found data for the stadiums throughout the United States, but thought it would be interesting to focus in on North Carolina. It's interesting to see the trend of bigger capacties and just more stadiums near the center of the state near the triangle area, and less as you get farther away.
